install:
1. place dewm_hard_surface_assets.zip in your "team fortress 2" folder, i.e. \steamapps\common\team fortress 2
2. extract files

manual install:
1. open dewm_hard_surface_assets.zip
2. extract *.vmt and *.vtf into: \steamapps\common\team fortress 2\tf\materials\dewm
3. extract model files into: \steamapps\common\team fortress 2\tf\models\models\props_dewm <<--- note the models\models folder
4. extract .vmf file into \steamapps\common\team fortress 2\tf\maps
